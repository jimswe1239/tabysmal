To use this website, paste your tab in the tabs section. Make sure that you only enter one row of tab at a time (so don't paste a whole song, just the riff you're trying to play.)

If you try to transpose too low, you'll get question marks, meaning that you're trying to play negative frets. Currently, I don't have a fix for this, but I'm working on it.

Notes does nothing now, but I'm planning on making a similar app which outputs the names of the notes for people looking to play tabs on melodica or piano or whatever other instrument.
